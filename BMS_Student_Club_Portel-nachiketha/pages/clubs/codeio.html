CODE IO

President and head of BMSCE Codeio club

CodeIO is a technical community and club within the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, of BMS College of Engineering. The vision of CodeIO is to promote the exploration and development of technical skills in students. The outreach and work of CodeIO encompass three verticals: facilitation of Hackathons, workshops, and technology training sessions, by the CodeIO Research and Development (R&D) wing, hands-on competitive coding sessions, and preparation for campus placement tests and interviews, by the CodeIO Competitive Coding wing, along with the undertaking of various web application development projects within the institution like the Institutional Elective Portal and Placement Portal, by the CodeIO Development wing.

Objectives of CodeIO

  • Imbibe the ability among students to work as individuals and in groups during projects.
  • Contribute to open source projects, build websites and applications, provide technical support to organizations and various clubs on campus.
  • Work as a team and contribute to department and college level projects and make the club a better community than ever before
